Q&A

What makes a good designer?

A good designer knows they must understand the aim, what it is they are trying to solve, and who the problem belongs to.

You can make a design pretty in a pinch; but form must follow function.

I believe a designer must love fixing problems, and should relish that challenge.

Where do you find inspiration?

I find inspiration everywhere. Particularly in people, books, and film.

What is in your toolkit?

Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop and After Effects are my core tools. I also work with new-generation tools that use AI or natural language inputs to speed up idea-to-prototype cycles.

I'm tool-agnostic by design. The right tool is the one that helps you think better and test faster.

What is your management style?

I like to work in an environment where respect to others that are different is valued across all levels of a company.

Where you are encouraged to speak your mind when you see something that could be helpful.

No one knows everything and a fresh vision has always helped me bring light to a problem or one you didn't know existed.
